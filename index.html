The Crisis of Exclusion

The Triple Threat Holding Zimbabwe Back

Traditional centralized utilities ignore the periphery. Here is the economic and social toll of the digital barrier.

The Infrastructure Barrier

Traditional copper and fiber trenching requires massive capital expenditure. Legacy operators find it commercially unviable to lay physical cable to high-density "last mile" residential clusters.

Cost Prohibitive

The Access Gap

Monopolistic mobile data prices are a heavy economic tax. The average monthly data subscription absorbs 16% of average household income, pricing basic internet out of reach for families.

16% of Income

The Opportunity Chasm

Digital exclusion acts as a barrier to development. Without connectivity, students cannot access online learning, SMEs cannot tap e-commerce, and communities are locked out of the modern economy.

Locked Opportunities
Flagship Solution

Internet Yedu: Decentralized, Solar-Powered, Unstoppable

We've engineered an off-grid edge mesh network that bypasses the national electricity grid, delivering resilience directly to Harare CBD, Budiriro, Gweru, and beyond.

Digital Circular Economy

Providing the Water, Not Just the Pipe

Internet access is only the foundation. Collaboana deploys proprietary local platforms directly on the mesh, keeping transactions, learning, and transportation inside a digital circular economy.

Local Commerce

Ishambadzo

A hyper-local digital marketplace designed for SMEs, home industries, and informal traders. Users buy and sell locally, bypass national listing fees, and process payments without expensive gateways.

Education

Schoolit

A specialized learning platform delivering ZIMSEC-aligned digital curricula. Optimized to consume minimal bandwidth, Schoolit ensures students in high-density areas access rich educational content.

Transit

LiTS (Internet in Transit)

Transforms public transport corridors (combis and buses) into connected zones. Passengers enjoy high-speed, solar-cached connectivity on the move, turning standard commutes into productive windows.

Green Blueprint

Smart Zimbabwe 2030 & E-Mobility

A joint venture blueprint combining solar mesh infrastructure with electric mobility. By integrating electric vehicles (like BYD logistics fleets) with grid-independent charging stations, we create clean transport corridors.
